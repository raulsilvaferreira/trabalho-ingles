Problems caused by Loneliness

High pressure

Usually, lonely people have more chances to develop high pressure, this happens, because of many situations, like:

  • Eat types of food which doesn’t have a lot of vitamins, but a lot of salt.
  • High levels of cortisol in blood, because of the mental stress the body is obligated to control.

Diabetes

Emotional diabetes doesn’t exist, but it can cause indirectly, like eating too much candys, dysregulation with the hormones, etc.

Depression

Its associate with the empty feeling that most lonely people have, much more feelings and thoughts can make it even more sad, and annoying to most people in the world.

Stress and anxiety

The feeling of loneliness can make the brain understand that the body is under stress, producing the cortisol hormone.
